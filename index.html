About Me

My journey into graphic design started with a love for visual storytelling and creating impactful designs. Over the years, I've refined my skills through various projects, specializing in brand identity and social media design. I develop cohesive visual identities, including logos, typography, and color schemes, that reflect a brand's essence. In social media design, I create engaging posts, story templates, and campaigns to boost a brand's online presence.
